Teaching Philosophy

I believe in making mathematics and statistics accessible to all students through clear explanations, real-world examples, and patient guidance. My approach emphasizes:

  • Conceptual Understanding: Helping students grasp the underlying principles rather than just memorizing formulas
  • Applied Learning: Connecting theoretical concepts to practical applications in health sciences and research
  • Student Support: Creating an inclusive learning environment where students feel comfortable asking questions
  • Active Engagement: Encouraging students to work through problems and develop critical thinking skills
