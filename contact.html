Research Collaboration

I am always interested in collaborative research opportunities at the intersection of mathematics, statistics, and public health. If you are working on projects related to:

  • Longitudinal and survival data analysis
  • Mathematical epidemiology and disease modeling
  • Bayesian inference and statistical methods
  • Network analysis and surveillance strategies
  • Machine learning applications in healthcare
  • Maternal and child health research

Please feel free to reach out via email. I am particularly interested in interdisciplinary research that addresses real-world health challenges.

For Students

If you are a student seeking guidance in statistics, mathematics, or research methods, I am available for mentorship and tutoring. Whether you need help with coursework, research projects, or statistical analysis, please don't hesitate to contact me.

I offer support in areas including regression analysis, data analysis, experimental design, probability theory, and statistical computing using R, Python, and other software tools.
